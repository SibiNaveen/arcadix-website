Measure Competencies

TestDrive enables the location-independent, fast, reliable and cost-effective measurement of competencies.
The test organizer is completely relieved of the logistical effort involved in classic tests.

Complete

As a software suite, TestDrive supports the entire test process and can be integrated into existing CRM and ERP systems via interfaces.

Application
& Users

Our customers use TestDrive for self-checks, location assessments, performance comparisons, placement tests, psychotests and certification, or for strict tests.

Test Management

Authors create and manage tasks and tests independently. Test organizers manage users, plan executions with or without repetitions and have access to evaluations.

Learning
Content Management System

Tasks can be generated template-based with a few mouse clicks based on objects such as text, images, audio, video & flash.

Adaptive Testing

The level of difficulty of the tests automatically adjusts to the candidate's performance, based on probabilistic test theory.

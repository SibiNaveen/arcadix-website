TestDrive is a complete software suite for browser-based, multimedia and adaptive measurement of people's competencies. Furthermore, the software suite allows the management of system-externally tested competencies and the template-based generation of certificates.

TestDrive enables the location-independent, fast, reliable and cost-effective measurement of competencies.

Test Management

Authors create and manage tasks and tests independently. Test organizers manage users, plan executions with or without repetitions and have access to evaluations.

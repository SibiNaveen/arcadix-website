Several Hundred Thousand Tests Are Carried Out Every Year In Companies Customer Portal

Welcome!

TestDrive is a complete software suite for browser-based, multimedia and adaptive measurement of people's competencies. Furthermore, the software suite allows the management of system-externally tested competencies and the template-based generation of certificates. Performance comparisons over time and between people become possible.

The Significance

Several hundred thousand tests are carried out every year in companies, schools and careers advice centers on the basis of TestDrive. In the elementary and middle school sector, the product is used in Switzerland under the project names Stellwerk and Eprolog in 16 cantons and is a leader.

Renowned testing companies such as Multicheck and the federal government with the Swiss education server also rely on TestDrive.

TestDrive is currently available in version 3.0 based on the very latest technology. The product is constantly being further developed.
